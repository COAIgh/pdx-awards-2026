Award Structure

Three Families of Recognition

From exhibition floor excellence to corporate prestige to student innovation — we celebrate digital transformation at every level.

Tier 1 — Recognition

🔵 PDX Excellence

For PDX exhibitors only. Judged by booth performance, audience engagement, and innovation on the exhibition floor.

  • PDX Choice Award
  • Most Engaging Booth
  • Best Customer Experience
  • People's Choice
Tier 2 — Nomination

⚫🟡 Industry Awards

Paid entry for external companies. Corporate prestige across digital transformation, innovation, sustainability, and startup growth.

  • Digital Transformation of the Year
  • Most Innovative Use of Emerging Tech
  • Sustainability Through Digitalization
  • Rising Digital Startup of the Year
Tier 3 — Innovation

🟣 SUPRINOVA

For university students. Linked to IGNITE pitching ecosystem. Winners get investor-facing opportunities.

  • SUPRINOVA Innovation Award
  • SUPRINOVA Leadership Award
  • University partnership pipeline
  • Investor matching post-event
